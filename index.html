About Us

Impro UK Marine Service connects serious buyers with trusted shipyards and ready marine stock. Our current focus is South Asian markets, starting with Bangladesh. You can invest with local yard owners who welcome partners, or you can acquire a fully running shipyard subject to due diligence.

We export marine stock such as LNG grade aluminium plates, generators, large industrial motors, pumps and valves. All payments are handled by Impro Solutions UK Limited, a UK registered company since 2019.

Every deal follows strict ethics and authenticity checks, with full traceability from yard to export. Our full-time team is based near the shipyards in Sitakunda, Chattogram, Bangladesh, and can host yard visits, inspections and loading supervision.

Local Bangladesh:
  • Department of Environment clearances and ETP plan.
  • Fire Service and Civil Defence emergency and HSE setup.
  • Department of Explosives and hot work rules.
  • Trade License, IRC or ERC, VAT registration, factory or land papers.
  • Port and Customs processes for ship import and scrap handling.
  • Hazardous waste handling plan for oil, asbestos, batteries and more.
Outside and international requirements:
  • Basel Convention controls for any hazardous waste movement across borders and consent papers between exporting and importing authorities.
  • Hong Kong Convention guidance for safe and environmentally sound recycling of ships and ship specific Inventory of Hazardous Materials where needed.
  • EU Ship Recycling Regulation rules if the vessel is EU flagged or the buyer asks for EU compliant practice.
  • IMO and ILO safety codes for ship recycling, hot work permits, confined space entry rules and worker protection plans.
  • MARPOL and port waste rules for oils, sludge, bilge water, refrigerants and ozone depleting substances.
  • IFC and World Bank EHS guidelines used by many investors for environmental and social risk plans.
  • Third party inspection and due diligence by BV, SGS, Intertek or similar agencies for condition and compliance checks.
  • Import permits for scrap metals in the buyer country, radiation clearance where needed and pre shipment inspection if the buyer asks.
  • Export papers: commercial invoice, packing list, weighbridge slips, certificate of origin, test or grade papers and bill of lading.
  • Insurance and financial compliance: letter of credit or bank transfer papers, sanctions screening and end user statements if needed.
